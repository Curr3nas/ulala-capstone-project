Unlock at Level

There are many features available in Ulala: Idle Adventure, but like most games, you have to reach a certain level or stage in order to unlock those features. This is a list of all the features, and the level and/or stage that you unlock them!

  • Skills

    In order to unlock the skills feature your character must be level 6, and you have to complete the stage "Primitive Plains 6".
